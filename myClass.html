Trick #8:Default Background Transitions

背景切换动画

Config background transitions via the backgroundTransition option. This one's called "zoom". 

Reveal.configure({ backgroundTransition: 'zoom' })

Trick #9： Direct Jump

页面跳转

Page Number followed by enter to jump to a certain slide. Have a try. Type 5 followed by enter. Then type 10 followed by enter to come back.

Trick #10:Speaker View

There's a speaker view. It includes a timer, preview of the upcoming slide as well as your speaker notes.

Press the S key to try it out.

You can put your speaker notes here. They'll be hidden in your presentation, but you can see them if you open the speaker notes window (hit 's' on your keyboard).

Trick #11:Black out the slides
